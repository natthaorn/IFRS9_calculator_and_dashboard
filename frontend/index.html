ECL Stage Allocation & Coverage
ECL Segment Summary
IFRS 9 Regulatory Stage Criteria
Stage 1 (Performing): Loans that have not experienced a Significant Increase in Credit Risk (SICR) since origination. Requires 12-month ECL.

Stage 2 (Underperforming): Loans that have experienced a Significant Increase in Credit Risk (SICR) based on 30+ DPD, rating downgrade notches, or PD threshold exceedance. Requires Lifetime ECL.

Stage 3 (Non-Performing): Defaulted or credit-impaired loans (90+ DPD or Rating 10). Requires Lifetime ECL.
